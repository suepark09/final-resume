Hey there, my name is Sue! I'm passionate about building
immersive
and
beautiful
web applications through code and user-centric design.

Since graduating with a B.A. in Digital Technology and Culture from Washington State University in 2015, I started working as a graphic and UI/UX designer at ServerMonkey, where I design marketing material as well as creating prototypes and interactions for user interfaces. Other than dabbling in some HTML and JavaScript in college, software development as a career choice has never even crossed my mind- but that quickly changed.

I eventually started working closely with the developers on my team, which sparked my interest in programming. I started teaching myself using online courses such as FreeCodeCamp, CodeAcademy, and Udemy- and I was hooked! I love creating in general, and the fact that I was able to conceptualize an idea and bring it to life through code is so awesome. I realized that I needed more structure to my learning in order to achieve my goal, which was why I decided to invest in a coding bootcamp and enroll in DigitalCrafts to learn full-stack web development. I'm super excited to graduate in Jan 2020 and continue my journey as a software developer!

When I'm away from my computer, you can always find me shopping home decor items, reading webtoons, playing video games, or looking up new recipes on Pinterest! ^_^
